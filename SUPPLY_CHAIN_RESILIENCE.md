# 📦 Supply Chain Resilience: The Automated Pulse (v1.0)

> "Logistics is the physical manifestation of Love."

## I. The Automatic Trigger
The **Central Verification Hub** monitors the "Resource Equity" string. 
* **The Threshold:** If a district's "Need Pixel" stays red for more than 48 hours, the **Agape Engine** bypasses standard commercial logic.
* **The Action:** Funds are released from the **80/20 Buffer** to the nearest "Resonant Supplier."

## II. The "Shortest String" Protocol
Resources are moved via the **Shortest String**—meaning we prioritize local production to minimize the "Environmental Friction" (Carbon/Energy Waste). 

## III. Human Oversight (The Conductor)
While the AI handles the pulse, a **Human Conductor** (Verified via the Intellect Profile) can override the AI if the "Context Layer" (The Glow) suggests a better "Third Way."
