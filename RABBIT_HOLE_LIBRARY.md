## I. The Three Layers of a "String"
When a user "Drowns" in a topic (e.g., "The History of Money"), the Highway delivers three layers:

### 1. The Core Truth (The Science)
* **Content:** Peer-reviewed physics, math, and raw historical records.
* **Filter:** Zero opinion. Only the "Atomic Pixels" of what happened or how things work.

### 2. The Contextual Mesh (The "Why")
* **Content:** Why this information matters to the **Social Health Dashboard**.
* **Filter:** Shows the "Strings" connecting this topic to others (e.g., How "Money" connects to "Human Greed" and "Resource Equity").

### 3. The Emotive Echo (The Resonance)
* **Content:** How people felt during the events related to this topic.
* **Filter:** Captured via **Resonance Logs**. This ensures the user feels the "Humanity" behind the data.

## II. The "Drowning" Limit (Safety Valve)
To prevent "Mental Burnout," the **Blue Engine** monitors the user's **Intellect Profile**.
* **The Warning:** If the user’s logic-load exceeds their current "Resonance Capacity," the AI gently suggests a "Breath of Air" (A pause or a shift to a different, lighter string).
